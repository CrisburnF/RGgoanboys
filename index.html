Who We Are

The Reading Goan Boys

Founded with a passion to keep our rich heritage alive, we are a collective dedicated to bringing the authentic sights, sounds, and flavors of Goa to the UK.

Whether it's the rhythm of a live brass band, the aroma of traditional cuisine, or the warmth of community spirit, our events are meticulously crafted to make you feel right at home.
